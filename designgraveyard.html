Design Graveyard Collection

A poster designed for the AIGA art exhibition that featured rejected work titled Design Graveyard. The posters are the invitation to the exhibition and the request for artists to submit their work. The idea of “dead” work given new life at this exhibition is what inspired the art work of this poster.
