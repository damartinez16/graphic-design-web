HOME Annual Report

2018 Annual Report for the Home Operations Management Enterprise (HOME). The theme of the report was the idea of working together to create a better future. The design highlights this idea by using the gears that must work together as a whole to function. The interlocking of the gears represents the idea of unity in our community.
