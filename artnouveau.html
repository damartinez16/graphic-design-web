Art Nouveau in El Paso

Art Nouveau is an international style of art and architecture inspired by nature and organic forms. Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec was a French painter, printmaker, and illustrator who is known for his art showing the colorful and theatrical life of Paris. His work embodied Art Nouveau by creating illustrations that were formed through eccentric fantasy and mystic themes and his poster, Moulin Rouge: La Goulue, is what inspired this design of this fictitious advertisement for a gallery exhibition at The El Paso Museum of Art.
