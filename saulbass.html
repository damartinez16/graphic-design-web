Saul Bass in El Paso

Saul Bass was a prominent American graphic designer and Oscar-winning filmmaker. He was best known for his design of motion-picture title sequences, film posters, and corporate logos. His style of design was described as simple, using geometric shapes and a single dominate image to portray a powerful message. His designs were often hand-drawn to give it a casual appearance and that technique and his work for the movie poster The Two of Us is what inspired the image created for this poster. Hispanic culture is very prominent here in El Paso and so a Mariachi Musician was used as the dominant figure and a similar hand-drawn look was used for this advertisement for a fictitious gallery exhibition of Saul Bass’ work at The El Paso Museum of Art.
