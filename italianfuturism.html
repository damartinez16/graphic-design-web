Italian Futurism Catalog

Catalog created to promote a fictional exhibition of the work produced during the Italian Futurism movement in the El Paso Museum of Art. The catalog talks about the creation and goals of the Italian Futrists, as well as the different types of art forms created during this time, such as; graphic design, painting, architecture, and sculpture.
